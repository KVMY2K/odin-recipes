Grilled Salmon with Asparagus and Potato Puree

Fish and butter = Heavenly dishes

Fish is superior to meat in almost all cases, I will die on this hill. The reason I say almost, is because I adore stews like this. But this recipe is about fish, and in particular one of the most popular of them all, salmon. I will show you my recipe, and my favorite side pairs for this fantastic fish steak.

Ingredients

  • 1 piece of salmon fillet
  • 400g potatoes
  • 200g asparagus
  • 1 lemon
  • LOT of butter
  • 50 ml milk
  • olive oil
  • thyme
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Peel and cut up the potatoes, soak them, and put them in boiling water
  2. Have a glass of wine
  3. Cook the salmon over oil on all sides until brown, especially the skin
  4. When brown, turn down the heat slightly, add butter, thyme, and unpeeled garlic cloves
  5. Make sure the butter is plenty enough so you can baste the salmon in it
  6. Let it rest, then put the asparagus in the same pan and fry it with some extra butter
  7. When finished, let it rest, and smash the potatoes when ready
  8. Add salt and pepper (roughly grated is the best), the milk, and butter to your liking
  9. Plate it up, and add lemon juice to taste (I like to add it on my puree too)
